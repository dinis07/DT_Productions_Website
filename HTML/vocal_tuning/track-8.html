Drum Programming - Assignement 1

The assignment given contained 4 tracks which consisted of vocal, bass, guitar and a synth track. The task was to create a drum track for the given multi-tracks, to show our ability to create a a beat for any given track.

To create the track I started off with the hi-hats, giving the a back bone to start off with. The high hats were looped to the end at first so that I could start layering other instruments of the drum kit such as the kick drum.

Once the kick drum was layered I could start making some variations throughout the track, such as adding high toms, floor toms and cymbals. The whole drum track had a general similar beats in the verses, and different beats for the choruses. Throughout the track there are other instrument such as the cowbel was added to enhance the track.

Drum Programming - Assignement 1

Drum programme: Battery 4 Native Instruments

DAW: Protools 12

Software: Apple iMac
