About Us

I am a student at Bournemouth University learing to produce music along with sound engineering. My prefered genre is punk rock music however I do enjoy a varitey of genres such as blues, country, pop and occasionlly rap music.

Other than producing music i enjoy traveling, kitesurfing and working at events. Visit the galleray tab to view recording session and other events I go to. The gallery shows the facilities used when recoring. The website gallery will be updated weekly and advertisments for events in the area will be posted, as well as event in near citites.

DT Productions is an independent label where starting musicains can send their raw recordings to get mixed and mastered. In addition vocal tuning is another service provided as well as midi composition for drums or synthesizers. My Compositions are also available to listen to on the site and I am willing to send the session files of my compositions for production learning purposes
