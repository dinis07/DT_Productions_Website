DT Productions

Mixing & Mastering

The mixing and mastering section contains tracks which have been mixed and mastered by Dinis Tavares. The tracks were given me from a thrid party and asked to ensure the tracks are up to standards, and ready for listeners. All tracks were mixed and mastered using Pro Tools along with other plugins on a Apple iMac. When needed some tracks are re-recording by me if needed to provide the best possible track.

Vocal Tuning

The vocal tuning sections have all the track which were again provided to me by third parties, where some vocal adjustment were needed to be done. All vocal tuning is done on Cubase 4 or Melodyne. Sometimes I will re-record some tracks as a good quality track is needed to tune the vocals to.

Composition

This is my creative section, where I will compose either a full song, or a single track to accompany and existing track. A variety of programmes are used to achieve the end goal. Some software programmes used are Native Instruments, Battery, Kontact, FM8, Massive and many more.
